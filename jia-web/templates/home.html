What is JIA?

JIA (家; jyah) translating to “home” in Chinese, is a house supper club based in the Greater Seattle Area. JIA is not only a host to regularly-changing dinner menus but also an invitation to meet new friends through communal dining. Focused in Asian and New American flavors, we like to keep the boundaries of cooking at a minimum. If you seek a concentrated date with your community while enjoying a drink, listening to good music, and eating some tasty food together, our dinners are a perfect match.

Eat JIA, eat home.
