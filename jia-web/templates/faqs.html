Frequently Asked Questions

How to pay?

To secure your reservation at JIA, a 20% deposit of the fixed price is required. Once we receive your deposit, your spot will be confirmed. Remainder of payment will be collected at the end of the dinner either through cash or Venmo. Deposit is non-refundable.

Where is JIA located?

JIA resides in the suburbs of Renton, WA. Upon reservation booking our specific address will be disclosed.

What type of dining is a supper club?

A supper club can refer to an underground restaurant; an intimate and social dining experience for foodies away from the crowds while enjoying good music. While traditionally presenting multiple courses, beverages, and desserts, we also rotate family-style dining, where everyone at the table can partake in a shared feast together.

What is the maximum number of guests I can bring?

There are no set limitations on party size, as long as all guests can comfortably fit within the designated space, considering any booking restrictions for the night.

Do you offer menus for lunch?

For our private dining and catering events, we are glad to serve any time of day so long as our schedules align.

Why is there a deposit to RSVP?

To ensure a seamless dining experience and optimize our preparations, we tailor each dinner based on the expected number of guests. Collecting a deposit helps minimize last-minute cancellations, which can be disruptive and wasteful. By securing a reservation, we can devote ourselves into delivering an exceptional night to those who have chosen to join us.

Where can I park?

Because we are located in a residential neighborhood, we kindly request that you refrain from parking in front of the neighboring houses, ensuring their access remains unobstructed. Instead, there is ample street parking available along a long street behind the house. You can conveniently enter through the back fence gate, where a warm greeting awaits you.

Do you sell alcohol/drinks?

Our dinners currently operate on a BYOB (Bring Your Own Bottle) policy. However, please note that for specific dinners if drinks are provided, they will be listed in the corresponding menu.
