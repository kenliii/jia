JAY YUEN, CHEF

Frying eggs since age 9, cooking family holiday feasts since 14, Jay grew up in Arizona as a Chinese American being constantly obsessed with food. After running a catering business in high school, Jay entered the restaurant industry working Japanese cuisine full-time while in university and decided his career dream then.

Jumping at every opportunity to add to menu items and learn new roles, he quickly progressed his way to leadership positions at a young age. Jay then relocated to New York City to push further, working alongside Michelin-trained chefs and adopting Korean and French technique at James Beard-nominated LittleMad.

Now returning to Seattle on a break from traditional restaurants, Jay presents JIA as an outlet to those in the Greater Seattle Area that are not only hungry for food, but hungry for connection.

Find out more @jaycantcook

RICHARD NGUYEN, COMMIS CHEF

Born and raised in south Seattle, Richard grew up learning to appreciate his Vietnamese and Chinese cultures through the mixed home cooking of his mom. Helping her cook traditional dishes from various regions of Vietnam since he was 10, it wasn't till 2018 that Richard entered the restaurant world and began working in sushi where he and Jay met. After working for 5 years, Richard joins JIA to further push himself practicing different cuisines and techniques while testing dishes, expanding his interest in the world of culinary arts.

Richard's enthusiasm adds a dynamic and charismatic touch, elevating the dining experience for every guest. With his commitment to continuous growth and his curiosity to the craft, JIA is consistently a genuine space for all people to feel welcome.
